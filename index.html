Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service the porta-potties during long events?

Yes, we provide maintenance and restocking to keep units sanitary throughout the rental period.

Are your porta-potties environmentally friendly?

We use eco-safe chemicals and follow waste disposal guidelines that reduce environmental impact.

How many porta-potties do I need for my event?

This depends on your guest count and event duration. Paramount Porta Potty Rental provides consultation to ensure you have the correct number of units for comfort and compliance.

Can I rent for a long-term project?

Yes, porta-potties can be rented for weeks or months, with flexible maintenance schedules.

How often are porta-potties serviced?

Standard rentals include weekly servicing, but additional cleaning schedules can be arranged depending on the event or site size.

Yes, we supply standalone sink stations and units with built-in handwashing facilities.
